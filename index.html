Assistant Professor of Civil Engineering

I joined the Department of Engineering at Quinnipiac University as an Assistant Professor in the Fall 2014. I am building a fantastic new lab here for undergraduate teaching and research in structural engineering and materials.

My teaching interests are in structural analysis and design, sustainable construction materials, and project management. My research interests are aimed at understanding the behavior of porous civil engineering materials, including deterioration mechanisms in cement and mortar, microstructural modeling, cement characterization, repair methods for damaged concrete structures, and the development of sustainable civil engineering materials. I am also interested in engineering education research, especially methods on how to effectively integrate writing in the engineering curriculum.
